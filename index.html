About Us

We are a community-based non-profit organization established in 2025 to expand access to quality education, digital literacy, and vocational skills in underserved communities across Uganda.

DETF is registered with Iganga District Local Government and URSB, addressing youth unemployment, school dropouts, and limited access to technology.

Mission

To equip communities with digital, educational, and entrepreneurial skills.

Vision

To build digitally empowered and skilled communities.
